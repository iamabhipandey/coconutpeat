About Us CoconutPeat by Loche India

At CoconutPeat by Loche India, we provide premium, eco-friendly coconut-based products made for better gardening, farming, and sustainable growing. Our coco peat, coir fiber, mats, and grow bags are crafted with care to deliver consistent quality, excellent water retention, and healthier plant growth. We focus on natural, renewable materials that support both the environment and modern growers.

100% Natural & Eco-Friendly

Pure coconut-derived materials with zero chemicals, making products safe for plants, homes, and industries.

Sustainable & Renewable Resource

All products are made from renewable coconut husks, supporting green agriculture and reducing environmental waste.

Completely Biodegradable

All coconut products naturally decompose, making them perfect for eco-conscious homes and businesses.

Multi-Purpose & Versatile

Useful for gardening, landscaping, home décor, agriculture, mattresses, ropes, mats, handicrafts, and more.
